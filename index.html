Platform Environment Overview

Prepared for: YMCA of Southeastern North Carolina

As the YMCA of Southeastern North Carolina moves forward in its transition from a legacy CRM to Memberworks, you're not just changing software — you're modernizing the way your organization connects with members, manages programs, and powers your mission. To support a smooth and confident migration, Memberworks provides three distinct environments, each playing a specific role in your transformation. This document explains what each environment is, how it differs from the others, and what your team can expect along the way — so everyone always knows exactly where to work, what's ready, and what's coming next.

The Three Environments
🎨
Design Blueprint
Memberworks Platform Prototype

The platform prototype is the complete visual blueprint for the Memberworks platform — the full picture of what the finished product will look like and how it will work. Think of it as the architect's master plan. Everything built in Production originates here.

✦
Represents the complete, intended vision of the Memberworks platform in its entirety.
✦
Serves as the primary source of truth that drives all development decisions and feature specifications.
✦
Regularly updated with enhancements and new features, while its foundational structure remains ~90% intact.
✦
Useful for previewing upcoming features and understanding where the platform is headed.
✦
Not a live, functional system — it is a visual prototype only and cannot be used for real data entry or program management.
🎨 Design Reference Only
🚀
Live Platform
Memberworks Production Environment

The Production environment is your live, real-world Memberworks platform. Features are built using the platform prototype as the specification, rigorously tested by our team, and then released here. This is where your real data lives and where your members, staff, and programs will be managed day-to-day.

✦
The official, live system used for real operations — member data, programs, registrations, and more.
✦
Features are thoroughly QA'd by Memberworks before being released here, ensuring stability and reliability.
✦
All features are developed directly from the platform prototype specifications.
✦
Will continuously grow to match the full platform prototype as new features are developed and released on an ongoing basis.
✦
Data entry, program setup, website setup, and settings configuration begin here on June 1.
🚀 Live Operations — Real Data
🧪
Safe Practice Space
Memberworks Sandbox Environment

The Sandbox is your team's practice playground. It mirrors the Production environment exactly, giving your staff a safe, consequence-free space to explore features, test configurations, and build confidence — without any risk to live data or real member records.

✦
Mirrors Production — everything available in Production is also available in Sandbox at the same time.
✦
A safe, nonbreakable environment — explore, click, configure, and test without worry. Nothing here can harm your live system.
✦
Ideal for staff training, testing new configurations, and learning the platform before going live.
✦
Important: Data entered in Sandbox does not transfer to Production. Any setup work intended for live use must be re-entered in Production.
✦
Best used for learning and experimentation — not as a staging area for Production-ready configurations.
🧪 Safe Practice — No Real Data

Important Reminders
📅
June 1, 2026 is go-live day. Data entry, program setup, website configuration, and settings will begin in the Production environment on this date. Use the Sandbox before then to get comfortable with the platform.
⚠️
Sandbox data does not carry over. Any work done in the Sandbox — programs created, settings configured, data entered — stays in the Sandbox. Real setup work must be performed directly in Production.
🔄
The platform prototype evolves. Our team regularly adds enhancements and new feature designs to the platform prototype. Its foundational structure (~90%) remains consistent, but expect to see new additions as our platform vision expands.
🏗️
Production is a work in progress — by design. Not every feature in the platform prototype is available in Production yet. This is expected. New features are released continuously as they are built and QA'd. Refer to the timeline above for specifics.
